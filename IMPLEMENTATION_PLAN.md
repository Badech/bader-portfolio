## 🎨 Design Principles to Maintain

1. **Premium, minimal, modern** - Stripe-level clarity, Vercel-level polish
2. **Warm but highly professional** - Built to impress U.S. business owners
3. **Clean SaaS-like structure** - No clutter, intentional design
4. **Conversion-focused** - Every element serves a business purpose
5. **No template vibes** - Custom, boutique agency feeling
6. **Subtle animations** - Framer-level smoothness without overdoing it

## 🚫 Guardrails

- ✋ No visual clutter or template-like layouts
- ✋ No vague generic copy
- ✋ No fake metrics, clients, or testimonials
- ✋ No overly dark design unless it improves premium feel
- ✋ No sacrificing clarity for style
- ✋ No breaking current structure without reason
- ✋ No over-animation or flashy effects
